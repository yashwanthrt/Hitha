HITHA EEGA

Raajadharshini is a Tamil-born image maker whose style revolves around treading a line artfully between fashion, documentary, and portraiture. She is driven to capture a variety of raw emotions and stir a human connection through her striking colour palette and energetic imagery that reflect her roots.

Based between London and India.
